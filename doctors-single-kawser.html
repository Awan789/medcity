Biography

Dr. Kawser U. Ahmed, M.D. graduated and finished his residency in Internal Medicine in 2012. He then worked as a hospitalist at Kent County Memorial Hospital in RI for one year, where he taught internal medicine residents in-patient and out-patient.

Dr. Kawser then moved to New York City in September 2013 and joined NYC Health + Hospital as a faculty, he has been teaching interns, residents and medical students from NYIIT and St. George medical school.

Currently, Dr. Kawser is serving as an Assistant Professor of Medicine at Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai since 2015. Dr. Kawser has been working as part time primary care practitioner with Dr. Muhammed K. Rashid, M.D. at Multi Specialty Clinic since 2016. Dr. Ahmed is also affiliated with NSLIJ and NSNH as a community attending physician.

At Multi Specialty Clinic, we pride ourselves on providing the best possible patient care, while using today's latest medical technology and diagnostic testing. Our doctors and staff are committed to treating each patient as if he/she were a member of our own family. Multi Specialty Clinic is a state-of-the-art medical practice catering to the needs of men, women, and families throughout the Queens & Jamaica area.
