Primary Care Services

Our highly rated, board certified physicians are specialists in accurate diagnosis and treatment in a compassionate and friendly environment. Our Multi Specialty Clinic doctors are affiliated with some of the finest hospitals in New York including Queens General Hospital.

Physical & Routine Checkup

A physical examination is a routine test your primary care provider performs to check your overall health.

Job Physical Exam

A pre-employment physical ensures that the new hire is physically capable of performing the duties that will be associated with their job.

Sick visit/Same day visits

If you are not feeling well and need to be examined, Multi Specialty Clinic can accommodate you, typically on the same day.

Driving License Eye Exam

You must submit a vision test to the DMV within one year of your renewal. If you do not, DMV will take action against your license.

Medical Exam For Immigration

Immigration Physicals for all ages are conducted at MSC. For non-English-speakers, an interpreter will be provided at no charge.

Home Health Evaluation

A complete home care assessment is an essential step when coordinating care for an elderly loved one.
